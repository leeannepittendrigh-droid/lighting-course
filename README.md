# Commercial Lighting Essentials

Welcome to the **Commercial Lighting Essentials** training course. This program gives you the foundational knowledge to speak confidently with manufacturers, customers, engineers, and inspectors.

---

## Course Modules

### [Module 1 — Lighting Fundamentals](/module-1/)
*2.5 hours | No prerequisites*

The core language of commercial lighting: lumens, watts, efficacy, color temperature, CRI, fixture types, and technologies. Start here.

---

### [Module 2 — Advanced Product Knowledge](/module-2/)
*3–4 hours | Requires Module 1*

Reading spec sheets, LED drivers, dimming protocols, beam angles, photometric data, power factor, THD, and lighting controls.

---

### [Module 3 — Codes & Certifications](/module-3/)
*3–4 hours | Requires Modules 1 & 2*

NEC, NFPA 101 emergency lighting, energy codes (ASHRAE 90.1, IECC, Title 24), UL/ETL safety certifications, DLC, ENERGY STAR, IP and IK ratings.

---

## How to Use This Course

1. Work through modules in order — each builds on the previous
2. Take notes on anything you'd encounter in your specific role
3. Complete the quiz at the end of each module (**80% required to pass**)
4. Use the glossaries as quick-reference guides on the job

---

## Quick Reference

| Unit | What It Measures |
|---|---|
| Lumen (lm) | Brightness — total light output |
| Watt (W) | Power consumption — energy cost |
| Efficacy (lm/W) | Efficiency — lumens per watt |
| Foot-candle (fc) | Light level on a surface |
| CCT (Kelvin) | Color temperature — warm vs. cool |
| CRI (0–100) | Color accuracy |
